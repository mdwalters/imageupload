About

This is my very simple and ugly image uploader for the Scratch forums. It automatically uploads your images to a whitelisted image hoster without ads or required sign up. Just copy and paste the generated code in your post and you're set.

You may already know that CubeUpload, the main image hosting site people on the Scratch forums use, is on the brim of shutting down because of lack of funding. ImgBB is being considered as a replacement by the Scratch Team, but it hasn't been added yet because technically CubeUpload still works.

This website uses the ImgBB API to upload images directly and hosts the URL under Wikimedia, which is an allowed image hoster.
